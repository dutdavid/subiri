About Dr Subiri Obwogo

Dr. Subiri Obwogo is a medical doctor,specialist in pulic health medicine,Chevening Scholar,improvement advisor, farmer and consultant in health policy and systems strengthening.He has a Bachelor of Medicine and bachelor of surgery degrees from University of Nairobi and a Master of Science in Public Health Medicine from University of Nottingham school Epidemology and Public Health Medicine in the United Kingdom. He is the founder of kienyeji Kenya Ltd,a free-range poultry social enterprise and a knowledge management start-up that uses storytelling to foster learning and to build entrepreneurial knowledge and skills.He also offers poultry farming training.Dr Obwogo is also author of The Bombs That Shook Nairobi and Dar:A Story of Pain and Betrayal (1999). He worked as a clinical mentor and Deputy Chief of Party for PEPFAR ‘s AIDSRelief project in Kenya. His interests include health policy, improvement science, systems thinking, and chronic care models for integrating management of chronic diseases into primary care and policy planning. In the last three years, Dr. Obwogo has worked as Senior Quality Improvement Advisor with the ASSIST Project to provide technical support to the Ministry of Health in developing the Kenya Health Improvement Policy, Strategy and plan of action. He also contributes editorials in local newspapers.
